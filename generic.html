Biography

I am a Nigerian African-American minority student with a profound connection to my Yoruba heritage. Born in Rhode Island and raised in Maryland, my cultural background and personal experiences have significantly shaped my identity.

Currently, I am pursuing my education with a focus on web development. I have a solid understanding of basic web development concepts but face challenges in effectively applying these skills, particularly in front-end frameworks and JavaScript. Building projects from scratch can be daunting, and I often encounter creative roadblocks. Despite these challenges, I am dedicated to improving my skills and advancing my proficiency in web development.

In addition to my academic and professional pursuits, my Christian faith is a cornerstone of my life, providing guidance and strength.
